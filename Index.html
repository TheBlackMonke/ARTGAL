ABOUT ADJ'S PLACE

Our story

Adj's Place is a brand-new staycation in Imus, Cavite, famous for tourist attractions and events. It is a location that combines both historical importance and economic advancement.

CONTINUE

The owner Adjie, purchased the property in 2016; however, he chose not to live there since he preferred the city. Instead, he used it as a sleeping investment for years until he recognized that it was better to utilize the property, implemented automation, and began offering it to the public as an Air BnB. His credo is that if you have nothing to do, why not start a business?

Having a safe stay here is an excellent alternative for tourists seeking a superb holiday location in Cavite's capital city. Every time customers stay at our budget-friendly lodging, they have a delightful and secure vacation. Security measures are in place, and Amazon Alexa is installed, enabling voice commands to manage all of the equipment, including all air conditioners, TVs, washers, etc., and turning on the lights. Even the cooker and rice cooker respond to voice commands.
